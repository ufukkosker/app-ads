Your privacy matters to us

Overview

StackArt ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data through third-party services:

  • Device Identifiers: Used for advertising and analytics purposes
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and crash reports
  • Purchase History: Subscription status and transaction information
  • Advertising Data: Information about ads displayed and interactions

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics: For app usage analytics
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions

Photos

StackArt accesses your photos only when you explicitly select them for splitting. Photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Data Storage

All split photos are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your photos or any personal content.

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Delete app data by uninstalling the app
  • Request information about your data by contacting us
